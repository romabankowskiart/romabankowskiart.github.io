About the Artist

Roma
Bankowski
United Kingdom

My creative journey is a continuous search for the invisible — a translation of internal emotions, fleeting thoughts and the raw energy of nature into a visual language of texture and colour.

I work in acrylics and mixed media, building each canvas in multiple layers — palette knives, brushes and, when the work demands it, my hands.

I create work for collectors who understand that a painting is not decoration. It is a mirror, a conversation, a living presence in a room.
