The Work

Four Collections.

Each collection is a distinct chapter — a different territory of the inner life, explored through texture, colour and gesture.

Collection 01

The Soul's
Mirror

An unflinching exploration of the interior life — silence, suppression and the gaze turned inward. These works examine what we hide, what we carry, and the moment our internal strength becomes our greatest trap.

Rendered in deep darks and urgent reds, each portrait confronts the viewer as directly as it confronts its subject.

Collection 02

Strength
& Beauty

Feminine power examined through elegance, solitude and the quiet theatre of private life. Women alone with their thoughts — unapologetic, self-possessed, and beautifully present.

These paintings celebrate the interior life of women — not as they are seen, but as they are.

Collection 03

Silents

A meditation on stillness, solitude and the spaces between words. These works invite the viewer to pause — to find comfort in the absence of noise and the beauty of quiet contemplation.

From intimate monochrome studies to the breath-taking scale of "Longing for Light", this collection moves through every register of silence.

Collection 04

Whispers of
Nature & Light

Beneath every visible world lies another — landscape, motion and the unseen architecture of feeling. These works move between the figurative and the abstract, between earth and sky, city and nature.

Built in layers of acrylic and mixed media, each canvas rewards close looking — there is always more beneath what is first seen.
